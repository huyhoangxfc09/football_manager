ABOUT CLUB

Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Holloway, London, currently playing in the English Premier League, the top level league in the English football system. Arsenal have won 13 league titles, 14 FA Cups (a record), 2 League Cups, 1 League Cup of the Century, 16 Super Cups, 1 UEFA Cup and 1 Inter-Cities Fairs Cup. Arsenal are the club with the second most wins at the top level in English football history, and also the only English team to date to win the national championship with an unbeaten record over 38 rounds.
